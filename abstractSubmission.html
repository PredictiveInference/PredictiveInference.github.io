Conference on Predictive Inference and Its Applications

The conference will include a poster session on the afternoon of May 7, 2018. To submit a poster abstract, send a plain text email to predictiveinference@gmail.com on or before April 15, 2018. The message should include

  • the poster title,
  • the name of the presenting author,
  • a list of other authors (if applicable),
  • the affiliation of each author, and
  • the poster abstract.

If you are a new researcher* presenting a poster, you may apply for up to $500 of travel support provided by a conference grant from the National Science Foundation. To apply, please include in your abstract submission email the sentence:

I qualify as a new researcher and wish to apply for travel support.

Such support will be provided (in the form of reimbursement for expenses) to up to 20 new researchers. Women and members of underrepresented groups are especially encouraged to apply.

*For the purposes of this poster session, new researchers are defined as graduate students or those who have earned PhD degrees between 2013 and the time of the conference.
