Conference on Predictive Inference and Its Applications

The conference talks will be presented in Alliant Energy-Lee Liu Auditorium in Howe Hall on the campus of Iowa State University. Alliant Energy-Lee Liu Auditorium seats 240 and is equipped with state-of-the-art audio-visual resources. The conference breaks, meals, and poster session will be held in the Howe Hall Atrium, which is adjacent to Alliant Energy-Lee Liu Auditorium.

Driving time between The Des Moines International Airport and Iowa State University is approximately 45 minutes. Some options for ground transportation between the airport and Ames include

  • car rental,
  • Executive Express shuttle service,
  • Crown Limousine,
  • UBER, and
  • lyft.

Some lodging options within three miles of the conference venue include

  • Gateway Hotel,
  • Hilton Garden Inn,
  • Best Western Plus University Park Inn and Suites, and
  • Iowa House Bed and Breakfast.
